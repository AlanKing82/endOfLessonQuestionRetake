Health Effects

  • If you are work with asbestos and you smoke your risk of developing a lung disease is increased by more than 50 times the rate expected in non-smokers with no asbestos exposure.
  • Asbestos related diseases cause approximately 4500 deaths each year (this is rising), source: http://o-las.net/admin/cdt_screens/edit/www.hse.gov.uk/statistics/causdis/asbestos.htm
  • Single very large exposure can potentially kill, however small repeated exposure is of particular concern also.
