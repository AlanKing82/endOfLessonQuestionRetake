Health Effects

  • It can take anywhere from as little as 15 to over 60 years from initial exposure to the development of disease and the symptoms of cancer appearing.
  • This latency period means that many workers may become ill years after they leave a job or have been exposed.
  • Many do not remember when, or where, they were exposed to asbestos. Therefore it is very difficult to take court action as many people have had more than one employer.
