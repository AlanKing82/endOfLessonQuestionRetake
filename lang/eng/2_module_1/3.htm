Asbestos Dangers

Asbestos is only dangerous if you can breathe in, or ingest, the fibres (dust). Solid asbestos containing material is very low risk. It becomes high risk if it is damaged or the surface deteriorates so that fibres are released either by material falling off or people brushing against it.

CAR 2012 regulations indicate that almost all workers within the Construction Industry will come across ACM’s. These include (although not limited to):

  • General maintenance staff
  • Electricians
  • Plumbers
  • Gas fitters
  • Painters and decorators
  • Joiners
  • Plasterers
  • Demolition workers
  • Roofers
  • Shop fitters
