Asbestos Properties

Asbestos has a variety
of desirable properties such as:

  • High tensile strength
  • Stable at high temperatures
  • Resistance to chemicals
  • Low cost and abundant


Other factors include:

  • Good thermal, electrical and sound insulator
  • Long flexible fibres
  • Reinforcing and binding agent

However the very bad side is:

  • It does not rot so disposing of the material is a serious environmental concern.
