Asbestos Textiles

  • Asbestos textiles were manufactured for primary heat (e.g. insulation tapes and ropes) or fire protection uses (e.g. fire blankets, fire curtains, fire-resistant clothing).
  • Textiles were also used widely as a reinforcing material in friction products/composites.
