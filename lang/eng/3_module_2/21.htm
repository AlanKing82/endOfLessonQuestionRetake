Asbestos register example — Material assessment score

  • Each parameter is scored between 1 and 3
    • A score of 1 is equivalent to a low potential for fibre release,
    • 2 = medium, and
    • 3 = high
  • Two parameters can also be given a nil score (equivalent to a very low potential for fibre release).
  • The value assigned to each of the four parameters is added together to give a total score of between 2 and 12.
