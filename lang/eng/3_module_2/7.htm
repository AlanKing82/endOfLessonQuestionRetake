Desktop study/GAP Analysis:

  • A desktop study provides you with your first step to putting your business on track for full compliance with current legislation regarding asbestos – the Control of Asbestos Regulations 2012.
  • Taking the form of a gap analysis, the desktop study examines what you have done to date and identifies any shortfalls.
  • A simple schedule is then created to help you plan your activities and show your route to compliance with the current and planned asbestos legislation.

The study will:

  • Review all current asbestos information held by your company.
  • Clarify which procedures you already have in place for the management of asbestos.
  • Identify the steps you need to take to achieve full compliance with current asbestos legislation.
  • Provide a route map to compliance with easy to follow directions.
  • Advise you of the procedures or adaptations you must make to ensure that you can show you have taken all reasonably practicable steps to manage your company’s risk from asbestos.
