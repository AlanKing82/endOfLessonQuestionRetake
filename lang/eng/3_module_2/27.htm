Legal Framework

If you suspect asbestos to be present where you are working, either in material in place
or as fallen debris around you then:

  • Stop work immediately.
  • Inform those around you.
  • Everyone should leave the area and the area secured to prevent
    others from entering the area where asbestos is suspected.
  • Contact your line manager and await their further instructions.
